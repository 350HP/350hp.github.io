Churning Karma

Get your Churning Karma to check if you are eligible to post in the referral threads

How does it work?

  • Gets your comments on /r/churning from the past 6 months. Reddit only gives the last 1000 comments total so if you are a very active user, this might not be accurate
  • For each of your comments, check the comment score
  • If comment score>0, add (comment score-1) to your churning karma otherwise add -1.

Everything is fetched locally by your browser so don't abuse this by checking everyone's score. Doing so might cause reddit to place limits on you.
